CLASSES

6th grade
There is Science, Math, Language Arts, Social Studies and extra classes

There is 2 classes each day of either Science, Math, Language Arts, Social Studies and at the end of the day you have extra classes.

7th grade
There is Science, Math, Language Arts, Social Studies, Sports and extra classes

We have to attend all the classes from Science, Math, Language Arts, Social Studies and we have sports to choose like tennis, volleyball, track, etc and extra classes at the end of the day.

8th grade
There is Science, Math, Language Arts, Social Studies, Sports, Music and extra classes

We have to attend all the classes from Science, Math, Language Arts, Social Studies and we have sports to choose like tennis, volleyball, track, etc and we have music where you can sing or play an instrument and extra classes at the end of the day.
